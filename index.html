Frequently Asked Questions

What are the "Big Five" species found in Africa?

The African lion, African elephant, Cape buffalo, African leopard, and rhinoceros are known as the "Big Five."

What is the importance of Lake Chad in West Africa?

Lake Chad is a crucial water source for the region, supporting livelihoods and biodiversity.

What is the Great Rift Valley, and where is it located?

The Great Rift Valley is a geological feature stretching from Lebanon in Asia to Mozambique in Southeast Africa.

Which famous ancient civilization was located in present-day Sudan?

The Kingdom of Kush.

Which desert is located in southern Africa and is known for its unique sand dunes?

Answer: The Namib Desert.

Which African country is known for its unique rock-hewn churches in Lalibela?

Ethiopia.

What is the largest wildlife reserve in Africa?

Selous Game Reserve in Tanzania.

Which African city is considered the cultural capital of the continent?

Answer: Dakar, Senegal.
